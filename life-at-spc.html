Admission &Fee Committee

Screening applications of allotted candidates by DOST. Guiding students to select the second languages. Fee committee helps students and parents in understanding the fee structure

Academic & Examination Committee

Academic committee helps students to select the SEC courses. Examination committee undertakes Internal Examinations.

Internal Quality Assurance Committee

Quality Assurance of the college is looked upon by IQAC.

Time Table Committee

Time table committee frames the Time Table for the whole college.

Library Advisory Committee

This committee takes cares off the library books and journals. It is mostly funded by UGC.

UGC Advisory Committee

Proper utilization of the funds released by UGC and utilization certificate submitted to UGC is done by this committee. From UGC IV plan till date the college is being funded by UGC.

Research Committee

The committee guides faculty members in preparing research project proposals. Encourage research scholars to present and publish papers in International and National journals.

Magazine Committee

“SARDAR” the magazine of our college is published annually with student editors.

Handbook Committee

The handbook of the college is issued to I year students who just enter the college.

Disciplinary Committee

The discipline of the students in the college premises is looked after by this committee.

Scholarship Committee

The committee scrutinizes the applications of students for various welfare schemes which are sanctioned by the State Government. The scholarships are disbursed to the students directly into their accounts.

Placement Committee

Placement Committee will invite all reputed firms from different sectors of like software and industries. They conduct different types of selection and hire the students based on their skills.

TASK

The TASK committee conducts training programs for students to enhance their skills.

NSS Committee

National Service Scheme (NSS) aim is “NOT ME BUT YOU” and Personality Development through community service. It is a platform to exhibit the hidden talents of students and gives ample opportunities to develop leadership qualities.

Grievance Committee

The committee handles the problem faced by staff and students.

Red Ribbon Club

Under the NSS units RRC will bring awareness among the adolescent teenagers about the AIDS.

Health & Hygiene Committee

Maintaining good Sanitation and Greenary in Campus and Promoting Hygiene and health of Students

Literary Activities Committee

The committee conducts competitions to students in all languages to bring out the academic talent and also encourages them to participate competitions conducted by other institutions.

Cultural Activities Committee

The committee conducts competitions to students in all activities which are extracurricular to bring out the talent and also encourages them to participate competitions conducted by other institutions

Functions & Programs Committee

Every function of our college is organized by this committee.

Anti ragging Committee

The committee ensures students that the premises are ragging free zone.

Eco Club

The committee encourages eco friendly environment promoting Swacch Bharath.

Consumer Club

Bringing awareness among the students about the product pricing, quality and how to approach the grievance cell regarding consumer protection.

English Forum

The forum encourages students to converse in English fluently by conducting debates, group discussions.

Website Committee

Day today events and information regarding the activities in the college with photographs are uploaded by this committee.
