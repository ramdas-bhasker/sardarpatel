AUDITORIUM

The auditorium in the college has a capacity of 700 members.

The seminar hall is utilized for conducting extension lectures which is an Ac hall.

The cellar parking in the college has a capacity of 30 four wheelers and 100 two wheeler vehicles.

  1. TATA AIG personal accident policy of Rs. 1, 00,000/- .
  2. Earning parent benefit: A unique benefit of Rs. 1, 00, 000/- given to the management of the institute to ensure quality education to the student of the institution in case of his/her income-earning parent meets with an accident, resulting in death.

STUDENTS ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE

College awards gold medals to students who excel in academics. 9 gold medals sponsored by management and 2 Gold medals sponsored by retired faculty members (Sumathi gold medal for topper in B. Sc, M.P.C sponsored by Dr. V. Sita, Retd. Faculty in English and P. Rama Krishna Reddy Gold medal for topper in M. Sc (Chemistry) sponsored by Dr. P. Chandrasekhar Reddy, Retd. Faculty in Chemistry .
